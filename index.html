Intimate Wildlife Observation on the Skagit and Nooksack Rivers

Our motorless drift boat accommodates just two guests (plus your water-savvy pup!) for peaceful, educational floats. No gas, no noise—just the sounds of the river and expert naturalist guidance as we explore the unparalleled beauty of Bellingham's waterways.

What You'll Experience

Eagles & Raptors

During salmon season, observe bald eagles, golden eagles, and other birds of prey along the riverbanks. Bring your camera for once-in-a-lifetime photographic opportunities as these powerful predators hunt and perch mere feet away.

Forest Floor Ecology

Discover the hidden world beneath the canopy—mushrooms, moss, lichens, and the decomposers that power the Pacific Northwest ecosystem. Each float includes opportunities to explore the riverbanks and learn about the forest's intricate web of life.

Salmon Migration

Witness the incredible journey of Pacific salmon returning to their natal streams to spawn. Learn about their vital role in the ecosystem and the challenges they face, from ocean to river and beyond.

Diverse Wildlife

From Pacific newts to black-tailed deer, river otters to osprey, every float offers unexpected wildlife encounters. The riverbanks are alive with the creatures that call the North Sound home.

Hands-On Instruction

Our Fly Fishing 101 course provides comprehensive instruction for all skill levels. Master casting techniques, learn to read water, understand fish behavior, and gain confidence handling a drift boat. All equipment provided.

Conservation Mission

A percentage of all tour proceeds directly supports local environmental organizations protecting North Sound waterways. Your adventure contributes to the preservation of these fragile ecosystems for future generations.
